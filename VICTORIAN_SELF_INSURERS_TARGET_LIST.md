## Critical Insight: Existing Systems Challenge

**User Observation**: "It's likely they have their own large claims management systems in place already"

**Strategic Implication**: Large self-insurers have significant existing technology infrastructure. Preventli must position as:
1. **Integration partner** (not replacement system)
2. **Compliance enhancement** layer
3. **AI-powered analysis** overlay
4. **Specialized WorkSafe expertise** supplement

## Revised Business Development Strategy

### Tier 1: Integration-Ready Targets (Highest Priority)

**Target Profile**: Organizations with existing systems but limited WorkSafe AI capabilities

**Approach**: "Compliance AI Layer" positioning
- Integrate with existing HRIS/claims systems via API
- Provide WorkSafe-specific compliance intelligence
- Offer AI-powered decision support without system replacement
- Position as regulatory risk mitigation tool

**Pricing Strategy**: Integration-friendly model ($2K-8K/month + implementation)

**Key Value Props**:
- "Enhance your existing system with WorkSafe AI compliance"
- "Reduce regulatory audit risk without system disruption"
- "AI-powered complex claims early identification"

### Tier 2: System Modernization Opportunities

**Target Profile**: Organizations with legacy/limited systems ready for upgrade

**Approach**: "Modern Compliance Platform" positioning
- Full-featured Preventli deployment
- Migration from legacy systems
- Complete workflow transformation

**Pricing Strategy**: Platform licensing ($5K-15K/month + professional services)

### Tier 3: Greenfield Opportunities

**Target Profile**: Newer self-insurers or those with minimal existing infrastructure

**Approach**: "Complete Compliance Solution" positioning
- Full Preventli implementation
- All features and capabilities
- High-touch onboarding and support
