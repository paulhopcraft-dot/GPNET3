The Problem

WorkCover is complex.
Most businesses handle it alone.

Large companies have dedicated return-to-work coordinators. Everyone else wings it — and one missed obligation can turn a minor claim into a six-figure liability.

⚠️
The legislation keeps changing
WorkCover obligations differ by state, and they change. Most employers find out they've breached something only after the insurer flags it — by which point it's already expensive.
📋
RTW plans are a minefield
Return-to-work plans have strict timelines and requirements. Get one step wrong and you're exposed. Most SMBs don't know where to start, let alone how to stay compliant through recovery.
💸
Consultants cost $150–$300/hr
When you do call a WorkCover consultant, the meter starts the moment you pick up the phone. For businesses with 1–2 claims a year, it's hard to justify — so most just don't get help.
