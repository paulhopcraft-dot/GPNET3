# Preventli Lifecycle Marketing Strategy v2.0

*Strategic Pivot: WorkCover Compliance → Complete Employee Health Lifecycle Platform*

## Brand Positioning

### Brand Promise
**"Your workers' health covered from day one to goodbye"**

### Positioning Statement
Preventli is the AI-powered occupational health intelligence platform that transforms reactive injury management into proactive health lifecycle management. For employers who want to predict and prevent workplace injuries while ensuring complete compliance documentation throughout the employee journey.

### Brand Personality
- **Predictive Intelligence**: See problems before they happen
- **Lifecycle Focused**: Complete employee journey coverage
- **Risk Prevention**: Proactive vs reactive approach
- **Compliance Shield**: Legal protection through documentation

## New Market Framework

### Target Market Expansion
**From:** High-injury industries only (construction, manufacturing)
**To:** ALL employers with occupational health responsibilities

### Market Segments

#### Primary: Large Employers (500+ employees)
- **Pain:** High workers comp premiums, injury liability exposure
- **Value:** Predictive prevention, premium reductions, liability protection
- **Budget:** $50-200 per employee annually

#### Secondary: Medium Employers (100-500 employees)  
- **Pain:** Manual health tracking, compliance complexity
- **Value:** Automated lifecycle management, audit protection
- **Budget:** $30-100 per employee annually

#### Tertiary: High-Risk SMBs (50-100 employees)
- **Pain:** Individual injury cost impact, regulatory burden
- **Value:** Early detection, compliance automation
- **Budget:** $25-75 per employee annually

## Campaign Strategy

### Campaign 1: "The Hidden Cost of Hiring" (Pre-Employment Focus)

**Objective**: Generate awareness of pre-employment health screening value

**Creative Concept**: Calculator showing cost of wrong job-person fit

**Tactics**:
- Interactive ROI calculator: "Cost of hiring wrong person for physical role"
- LinkedIn carousel: "5 signs you're hiring injury-prone workers"
- Webinar: "The $180,000 mistake: Wrong person, right role"
- Case study: "How proper screening saved $2.3M in claims"

**CTA**: "Calculate your hiring risk" → Demo

### Campaign 2: "Predict Don't React" (Current Customer Expansion)

**Objective**: Upsell existing customers to full lifecycle management

**Creative Concept**: Crystal ball / prediction metaphor

**Tactics**:
- Email sequence to existing 174 customers
- "Upgrade your injury management to injury prevention"
- Free lifecycle assessment for current customers
- Success metrics from pilot programs

**CTA**: "Free prediction demo" → Upsell meeting

### Campaign 3: "The Complete Story" (Enterprise Focus)

**Objective**: Position lifecycle management for large employers

**Creative Concept**: Employee journey timeline

**Tactics**:
- Executive briefing: "The Future of Occupational Health"
- ROI presentation showing lifecycle cost benefits
- Industry benchmark reports
- Partnership with insurance brokers

**CTA**: "Lifecycle audit" → Enterprise meeting
