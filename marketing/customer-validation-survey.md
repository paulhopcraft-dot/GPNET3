## Survey Follow-up

### Immediate Response Triggers

**High Interest (Q6 score 8-10 + Q10 "Yes definitely"):**
→ Personal call within 24 hours
→ Pilot program invitation
→ Early access to new features

**Medium Interest (Q6 score 5-7 + Q10 "Yes but need details"):**
→ Follow-up email with detailed concept
→ Case study examples
→ ROI calculator access

**Low Interest (Q6 score 1-4 or Q10 "No"):**
→ Thank you email
→ Continued focus on current injury management features
→ Re-survey in 6 months

### Success Metrics

**Validation Threshold:**
- 60%+ rate lifecycle concept as 7+ valuable
- 40%+ willing to participate in pilot
- 30%+ willing to pay premium for prediction

**Go/No-Go Decision:**
- **Green Light:** >60% positive response + pilot participation
- **Yellow:** 40-60% positive, modify concept and retest
- **Red Light:** <40% positive, focus on current product

## Survey Distribution Plan

### Phase 1: High-Value Customers (Week 1)
- 20 largest customers by revenue
- Personal email from you
- Follow-up call if no response in 3 days

### Phase 2: Engaged Customers (Week 2)
- Active users of current platform
- Standard email from marketing
- One reminder after 5 days

### Phase 3: All Customers (Week 3)
- Remaining customer base

- SMS reminder for non-responders

### Target Response Rate
- **Phase 1:** 80% response rate (personal outreach)
- **Phase 2:** 40% response rate (engaged users)
- **Phase 3:** 20% response rate (mass email)
