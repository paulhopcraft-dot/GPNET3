## Step 1 — Value the problem (ROI first, price second)

### What does a WorkCover claim actually cost?

| Cost component | Typical amount |
|----------------|---------------|
| Weekly wage replacement (off work) | $1,000–$1,800/week |
| Medical/treatment costs | $5,000–$20,000/claim |
| Average claim duration | 6–14 weeks |
| **Total average claim cost** | **$15,000–$45,000** |
| Claims going to conciliation | Add $10,000–$30,000 legal cost |
| Wrongful termination / adverse action | Add $50,000–$200,000 exposure |

### What does non-compliance cost?

| Breach | Consequence |
|--------|------------|
| Missing 28-day RTW obligation | WorkSafe improvement notice, premium loading |
| Failure to provide suitable duties | Fines up to $33,000 (WIRC Act 2013) |
| Premium loading for poor claims history | 20–60% on base premium — permanent |
| Unmanaged claim turning into conciliation | $15,000–$40,000 in legal/time costs |

### Preventli ROI calculation — SME (100 employees, 8 claims/year)

| Metric | Without Preventli | With Preventli | Saving |
|--------|-------------------|----------------|--------|
| Average claim duration | 10 weeks | 8.5 weeks (15% reduction) | 1.5 weeks × 8 × $1,200 = **$14,400** |
| Compliance breaches avoided | 2–3/year | 0–1/year | 2 × $5,000 avg = **$10,000** |
| Conciliation cases | 1 every 2 years | 1 every 4 years | $25,000 ÷ 2 = **$12,500/yr** |
| Premium trajectory | +10%/year | Flat or declining | $150K × 10% = **$15,000** |
| **Total annual benefit** | | | **~$50,000** |

**At $6,000–$12,000/year (our target price), ROI is 4–8x for an SME.**

### Preventli ROI calculation — Mid-market (400 employees, 25 claims/year)

| Annual benefit category | Estimate |
|------------------------|---------|

## Step 2 — Pricing Model

**Recommended: Flat monthly tiers by headcount band**

Reasons:
- Simple to quote and approve (no usage surprises)
- Scales naturally with org size
- HR Manager can approve lower tiers without CFO
- Annual billing with monthly option adds 20% uplift

---

## Step 3 — Proposed Tiers

### Tier 1 — Core (50–150 employees)
**$499/month** | $4,990/year (2 months free)

Includes:
- Full case management (unlimited cases)
- Compliance engine + action queue
- Medical certificate tracking
- RTW planning
- Termination documentation
- Reports + CSV export
- AI smart summaries
- Up to 5 Preventli users
- Email support

Best for: SME with HR Manager handling claims alongside other duties

---

### Tier 2 — Professional (150–500 employees)
**$1,199/month** | $11,990/year (2 months free)

Everything in Core, plus:
- Full assessment suite (injury, mental health, pre-employment, functional capacity)
- AI case chat
- Risk dashboard + predictions
- Audit log
- Up to 15 Preventli users
- Priority support + onboarding session

Best for: Mid-market with dedicated injury manager or HR team

---

### Tier 3 — Enterprise (500–1,000 employees)
**$2,499/month** | $24,990/year

Everything in Professional, plus:
- Multi-site / multi-entity support
- Custom compliance rule configuration
- Dedicated account manager
- Quarterly business review
- Unlimited users
- SLA-backed support (4hr response)

Best for: Large employer or employer group with complex claims portfolio

## Step 4 — Launch pricing strategy

**For beta clients (Weeks 3–4):** Offer 3 months free in exchange for:
- Structured feedback sessions
- Permission to use as case study (anonymised)
- Reference call for future prospects

**For first 5 paying clients (launch week):** Founding client rate
- 30% off first year (lock in at discounted rate, never increases)
- Positions them as partners, not just customers
- Creates urgency ("founding rate closes at end of April")
