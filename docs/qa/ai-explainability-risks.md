# AI Explainability Risks

**Generated:** 2026-03-24

## AI-RISK-001: AI Summary Not Correctable

**Severity:** HIGH
**Affected Workflow:** Smart summary / case analysis
**Risk:** AI-generated case summaries are displayed to case managers. There is no mechanism to flag inaccuracies, record corrections, or override AI conclusions. If AI asserts a worker is "likely to return to work within 4 weeks" and the clinician disagrees, there is no way to record the disagreement.
**Impact:** Case managers may act on incorrect AI analysis. No audit trail of human-AI disagreement.
**Recommendation:** Add "flag as inaccurate" / "add correction" mechanism. Store correction alongside AI output.

## AI-RISK-002: AI Email Drafts May Contain Inaccurate Case Details

**Severity:** MEDIUM
**Affected Workflow:** Email draft generation
**Risk:** AI generates email drafts using case context (compliance status, certificate dates, RTW status). If the AI hallucinates or misinterprets data, the draft may contain incorrect information. The two-step modal (configure → review) allows editing, but the user may not catch subtle errors.
**Impact:** Incorrect information sent to GPs, employers, or workers. Potential legal liability.
**Recommendation:** Highlight AI-inserted facts in drafts. Add confidence indicators for factual claims.

## AI-RISK-003: Pre-Employment AI Analysis → Clearance Level Without Human Gate

**Severity:** HIGH
**Affected Workflow:** Pre-employment health clearance
**Risk:** AI analyzes questionnaire responses and generates reportJson including a suggested clearance level. The `requires_review` flag catches uncertain cases, but cases where AI is confidently wrong would proceed without human review.
**Impact:** Candidates incorrectly cleared or rejected based solely on AI analysis. Safety risk for cleared-incorrectly candidates.
**Recommendation:** Require human review for all clearance decisions, not just AI-uncertain ones. At minimum, make AI clearance a suggestion, not a default.

## AI-RISK-004: AI Treatment Plan — No Clinical Sign-Off

**Severity:** HIGH
**Affected Workflow:** Treatment plan generation
**Risk:** Treatment plans can be generated by AI (`generatedBy: 'ai'`). Schema has `status: active` which implies the plan is in effect. No mandatory clinical review before activation.
**Impact:** AI-generated treatment plans may contain inappropriate interventions, incorrect durations, or miss critical clinical context.
**Recommendation:** AI-generated treatment plans should default to `status: draft` with mandatory clinician approval before activation.

## AI-RISK-005: AI Model Version Not Tracked Per Output

**Severity:** LOW
**Affected Workflow:** All AI outputs
**Risk:** `aiModel` field exists on treatment plans but not consistently on all AI outputs (summaries, email drafts, pre-employment analysis). Model version affects output quality and may be needed for audit.
**Impact:** Cannot trace quality issues to specific model versions. Cannot compare output quality across model updates.
**Recommendation:** Store model identifier on all AI-generated artifacts.

## AI-RISK-006: AI Confidence Score Without Threshold Policy

**Severity:** MEDIUM
**Affected Workflow:** Smart summary, treatment plans
**Risk:** AI outputs include confidence scores (0-100) but there is no defined threshold below which human review is mandatory. A low-confidence AI summary is displayed the same as a high-confidence one.
**Impact:** Users may trust low-confidence outputs equally. No safety net for uncertain AI.
**Recommendation:** Define confidence thresholds. Below threshold, require human review before display or add prominent warning.
