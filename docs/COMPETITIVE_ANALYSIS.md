## ğŸ” Competitive Landscape by Category

### 1. Pre-Employment Health (Fragmented Market)

**Traditional Occupational Medicine Clinics:**
- **Examples**: Sonic HealthPlus, Medibank Health Solutions, TerryWhite Chemmart Health
- **Model**: Physical clinic visits, paper-based processes
- **Limitations**: No digital integration, no predictive analytics, no job-fitness matching
- **Pricing**: $150-400 per assessment
- **Market Share**: Highly fragmented, no dominant player

**Digital Pre-Employment Platforms:**
- **International**: Concentra (US), HCA Healthcare (US) - minimal Australian presence
- **Australian**: Limited digital-first players, mostly traditional clinics with basic online booking
- **Gap**: No AI-powered job fitness matching or risk prediction

**Competitive Assessment: LOW THREAT**
- No comprehensive digital platforms
- Traditional players focused on compliance, not optimization
- Significant technology and process innovation opportunity

### 2. Employment Health Management (Mature but Limited)

**Workers Compensation Insurers:**
- **Examples**: WorkSafe Victoria, icare (NSW), WorkCover QLD
- **Model**: Regulatory compliance, claims management, reactive injury response
- **Limitations**: Reactive not predictive, compliance-focused, limited employer tools
- **Market Position**: Regulatory requirement, not competitive market

**Return-to-Work Providers:**
- **Examples**: EML, GallagherBassett, Allianz
- **Model**: RTW coordination, case management, medical provider networks
- **Limitations**: Post-injury focus, no prevention, limited technology
- **Pricing**: Funded through workers comp premiums
- **Market Position**: Established but technology-lagging

**Occupational Health Software:**
- **Examples**: RiskWare, Occ Health Manager, SafetyNet
- **Model**: Incident reporting, compliance tracking, basic case management
- **Limitations**: Not predictive, limited AI, focused on safety not health
- **Pricing**: $5-15 per employee per month
- **Market Position**: Utility software, not strategic platforms

**Employee Assistance Programs (EAP):**
- **Examples**: Converge International, AccessEAP, Workplace Options
- **Model**: Mental health support, counseling services, wellness programs
- **Limitations**: Reactive support, no health lifecycle tracking, no predictive capability
- **Market Position**: Established market but different use case

**Competitive Assessment: MEDIUM THREAT**
- Established relationships but limited innovation
- Technology gap creates opportunity
- Different value proposition (compliance vs optimization)

### 3. Exit Health Documentation (Greenfield Market)

**Current State:**
- **No dedicated platforms** for exit health documentation
- **Manual processes** through occupational medicine clinics
- **Limited liability protection** offered to employers
- **No integration** with pre-employment or employment data

**Competitive Assessment: NO COMPETITION**
- Completely greenfield market
- Significant first-mover advantage opportunity
