# Insurance Carrier Target Analysis for Preventli Case Management Platform

## Executive Summary

The insurance industry is experiencing a critical digital transformation period, with 68% of insurance companies identifying legacy systems as their biggest obstacle to digital transformation. This presents a significant opportunity for Preventli's proven case management platform to address high-volume claims processing needs across multiple carrier segments.

## Target Carrier Analysis

### Tier 1: Prime Integration Candidates (High Volume + High Technology Need)

#### **1. Travelers**
- **Market Position**: Largest workers' compensation carrier ($3.8B+ premiums)
- **Technology Status**: Advanced with TravComp system but seeking modern integrations
- **Integration Opportunity**:
  - High claim volumes requiring automated triage
  - Sophisticated investigative claim unit that could benefit from AI-powered case management
  - Existing technical infrastructure ready for API integration
- **Revenue Potential**: $2-5M ARR (enterprise contract)

#### **2. The Hartford**
- **Market Position**: Top 3 carrier with 105,000+ business customers
- **Technology Status**: Digital platform "My Workers' Comp Connection" but limited case workflow automation
- **Integration Opportunity**:
  - Massive provider network (1M+ medical providers) needing coordination
  - 65,000 pharmacy network requiring automated management
  - Strong return-to-work focus aligning with Preventli's lifecycle approach
- **Revenue Potential**: $1.5-3M ARR

#### **3. Chubb**
- **Market Position**: $2.9B workers' compensation premiums in 2024
- **Technology Status**: Advanced medical oversight but manual case management processes
- **Integration Opportunity**:
  - 20+ year specialization in workers' comp medical management
  - 100% medical bill review requiring automated workflow
  - Pharmacy benefit management integration potential
- **Revenue Potential**: $1-2.5M ARR

### Tier 2: Strategic Growth Targets (Mid-Market + Technology Forward)

#### **4. AmTrust**
- **Market Position**: $3.37B workers' comp premiums (2024)
- **Technology Need**: Modernizing legacy claim processing systems
- **Integration Value**: Mid-market focus with standardizable workflows

#### **5. Zurich North America**
- **Market Position**: 100+ year market presence, established processes
- **Technology Need**: Digital transformation of century-old workflows
- **Integration Value**: International carrier needing scalable solutions

#### **6. Liberty Mutual**
- **Market Position**: Major multi-line carrier with workers' comp focus
- **Technology Need**: Unifying case management across multiple lines of business
- **Integration Value**: Cross-line integration opportunities

### Tier 3: Emerging Opportunities (Specialty + Regional)

#### **7. State Insurance Funds** (NYSIF, etc.)
- **Market Position**: Regional monopolies with guaranteed volume
- **Technology Need**: Government-mandated modernization initiatives
- **Integration Value**: Stable, long-term contracts with public sector pricing

#### **8. Self-Insured Employers**
- **Market Position**: Large corporations managing own workers' comp
- **Technology Need**: In-house case management capabilities
- **Integration Value**: Direct-pay model with higher margins

## Technology Pain Points Alignment

### Legacy System Modernization
**Carrier Challenge**: 68% of insurers identify legacy systems as primary digital transformation obstacle
**Preventli Solution**: Modern API-first architecture with proven integration capabilities

### Claims Processing Inefficiencies
**Carrier Challenge**: Manual claims handling creating bottlenecks and processing delays
**Preventli Solution**: Automated intake, AI-powered triage, and workflow optimization

### Customer Experience Gaps
**Carrier Challenge**:

- 65% prefer mobile apps; 41% rate current apps as "poor"
- 82% want self-service; 56% report inadequate tools

**Preventli Solution**:
- Employee lifecycle portal with real-time status updates
- Mobile-first case management interface
- Self-service capabilities for claimants and employers

### Integration Complexity
**Carrier Challenge**: Difficulty creating connected ecosystems for digital transformation
**Preventli Solution**: Proven multi-tenant architecture with existing integrations (Freshdesk, Claude AI, medical providers)
