## üìä SAAL REVENUE MODEL FRAMEWORK

### Traditional SaaS Problem
- **Per-seat pricing** limits revenue growth
- **Feature-based** doesn't align with customer ROI
- **Subscription fatigue** - customers resist more tools

### SaaL Solution  
- **Per-task pricing** scales with customer success
- **Outcome-based** directly tied to labor cost savings
- **ROI transparent** - easy to calculate worker replacement value

### Pricing Structure by Worker Type

#### **Voice Workers (GoConnect)**
- **Per Call:** $0.25 - $2.00 depending on complexity
- **Monthly Base:** $200/month for 24/7 availability
- **Volume Tiers:** Discounts at 1000+ calls/month

#### **Avatar Workers (GoAssist)**
- **Per Session:** $3 - $25 depending on complexity/duration
- **Monthly Base:** $500/month for specialized knowledge
- **Success Fee:** % of deals closed or assessments completed

#### **Compliance Workers**
- **Per Document:** $1 - $10 depending on complexity
- **Monthly Base:** $300/month for ongoing monitoring
- **Audit Success Bonus:** One-time fee for clean audit results
