## üí∞ BUSINESS MODEL TRANSFORMATION

### **Current Revenue Model Issues:**
- Limited by number of employees at customer companies
- Customers question software value without clear ROI
- Difficult to expand revenue beyond initial subscription
- Competing with numerous health management platforms

### **New SaaL Revenue Model:**
- Revenue scales with customer business growth
- Clear ROI through direct labor cost replacement
- Multiple expansion opportunities (more workers, more tasks)
- Unique market position as labor replacement provider

### **Pricing Strategy Shift:**

#### **Phase 1: Hybrid Transition**
- Existing customers: Grandfather current pricing + worker fees
- New customers: Pure worker-based pricing
- **Example**: $15/employee base + $25/worker-task performed

#### **Phase 2: Pure SaaL**
- All customers: Worker deployment model
- **Basic**: $500/month base + $5/task
- **Advanced**: $1000/month base + $3/task (higher volume discount)
- **Enterprise**: Custom worker fleet pricing

#### **Phase 3: Success-Based**
- Base worker deployment fee + % of documented savings
- **Example**: $300/month + 20% of demonstrated labor cost reduction
- Aligns pricing with customer success
